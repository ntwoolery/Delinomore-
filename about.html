History of our restaurant

We have been opening in Seattle for 10 years. I was an immigrant from Hong Kong and I owned a deli restaurant in Hong Kong before I came here. Hence, I brought my passion of making good food to the US and started Delinomore. We tried to combine different cuisines in our menu so that our customers can enjoy special good food at a time.
